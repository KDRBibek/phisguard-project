PHISGUARD - Inbox

Inbox Tips

  • Check the sender and the subject for unusual wording.
  • Be cautious with emails that create urgency (e.g., “Act now”, “Account locked”).
  • Watch for requests for passwords, OTP codes, bank details, or personal info.
  • Look for spelling/grammar issues or strange formatting.
  • If unsure, open the email first and inspect it before clicking.
  • Use Report as phishing if it seems suspicious.

This is a safe simulation—take your time and learn.
