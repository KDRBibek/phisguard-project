Email Inspection Tips

  • Does it ask for passwords, OTP codes, bank details, or personal info?
  • Is it using pressure/urgency (“within 24 hours”, “immediately”)?
  • Is the greeting generic (“Dear user”) instead of your name?
  • Are the details vague (no order number, no proper signature, no contact info)?
  • Does it try to trigger fear/reward (“account suspended”, “refund available”)?
  • In real life: hover over links to check the destination domain.
